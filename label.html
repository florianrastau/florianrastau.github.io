My highlight at Corning Life Science was devising and implementing a solution for labeling 25ml bottles on three sides. Most of the work here was done automatically. However, one specific batch of 25ml bottles needed a long sticker that was wrapping around the bottle. This was done manually and took days for every batch.

After doing some research, I realized that the labeler we were already using could solve this issue with some modifications. My college and I built the foam attachment and in tandem with a vertical belt made the bottles spin and pressed the labels against every side of the bottle. For this improvement I was awarded the iKaizen of the First Quarter.
